What is ClassyFi?

ClassyFi is an in-browser photo classification program featuring real-time integration with Google Sheets for dynamic multi-user categorization and data-management. With an easy-to-use interface and open source customizability, ClassyFi is the optimal tool for manually classifying large photo datasets. Click here for an example of the classification interface.

Want more info? Sign up now to get started or check here to contact me and read the ClassyFi FAQ

What can I use this for?

Licensed through GPL-3 open sourced code, ClassyFi is customizable to fit your research needs. However, ClassyFi was built for maximum compatibility with FLICKR GeoSearch, a comprehensive app that executes geospatial photo searches across the entire Flickr photo community. FLICKR GeoSearch opens the door for anyone to access and analyze social media big data. However, any dataset capable of being stored on a Google Sheet document is compatible! Feel free to fork the master branch to build your own Custom ClassyFi or reach out to me here to see how I can help.

ClassyFi is currently being used in active research projects in the fields of botany and geography.

Getting Started

Working with ClassyFi is easy!


Classic ClassyFi

built-in methodologies

  1. Create an account here
  2. Follow instructions in the Dashboard to share your document with ClassyFi.
  3. Add your sheet through the Dashboard
  4. Get ClassyFi-ing!

Custom ClassyFi

custom methodologies

  1. Leverage the power of open source! Fork the master branch
  2. Create your own Custom ClassyFi using Python (Flask, Jinja) and some simple pre-templated HTML / CSS + JavaScript. Feel free to reach out to me to see how I can help
  3. Get ClassyFi-ing!
